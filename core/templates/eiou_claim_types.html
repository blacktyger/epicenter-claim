Are your EPIC-001 and EPIC-002 balances different?

EPIC-002 balance does not reflect any deposit to Vitex done after 12 March. The EPIC-002 token only reflects EPIC-001 balance BEFORE 12 March.

Balances on your account are compared in order to calculate the EIOU value. Each case must be manually investigated to validate Mimblewimble transactions correlated to your account.

Epic Cash coins purchased from the hacker account on Vitex after 12 March do not exist as these coins were produced illegitimately by the hacker. There is no option to claim the spoofed coins.

The USD value calculation of trades after 12 March is based on the EPIC/BTC and BTC/USD price when executed. This calculation represents the loss to be compensated with EIOU tokens.

  • Coins created (by mining or hack) after 12 March are removed from circulation due to rollback
  • Coins bought or sold after 12 March are no longer in circulation due to the rollback
  • Coins created before 12 March and sent to Vitex account after 12 March were returned to the source wallet due to rollback
  • Telegram username and Vitex address is required to submit claim
